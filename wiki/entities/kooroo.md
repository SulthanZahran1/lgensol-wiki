# KooRoo

## Snapshot
KooRoo is an LG Energy Solution in-house venture building battery swapping stations for electric two-wheelers. In this wiki, it is cross-referenced with [[baas|BaaS (Battery as a Service)]], [[bmts|BMTS (B.around Total Solution)]], and [[battery-management-system|BMS (Battery Management System)]].

## What To Remember
- KooRoo separates charging time from vehicle use by swapping depleted packs for charged packs.
- The sources connect KooRoo to BSS infrastructure, electric delivery culture, pack standards, and battery-life management.
- It is a service model where battery management, pack safety, station density, and user convenience matter as much as cell chemistry.

## LG Energy Solution Context
The source set treats this page as part of LG Energy Solution's battery ecosystem: products, partners, chemistries, process capability, software, and life-cycle management.
