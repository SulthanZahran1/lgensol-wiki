# Lithium Manganese Rich (LMR) Battery

## Snapshot
LMR batteries use lithium- and manganese-rich cathode chemistry to balance cost and performance. In this wiki, it is cross-referenced with [[lfp-battery|LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) Battery]], [[mid-nickel-battery|High-Voltage Mid-Nickel Battery]], and [[high-nickel-battery|High-Nickel Battery]].

## What To Remember
- Manganese can reduce dependence on expensive nickel and cobalt while supporting high-voltage cathode design.
- The engineering questions are voltage fade, cycle stability, gas generation, and electrolyte compatibility.
- The corpus links LMR to future product diversification and GM-related development work.
