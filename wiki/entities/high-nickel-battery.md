# High-Nickel Battery

## Snapshot
High-nickel batteries use nickel-rich layered cathodes to raise energy density for long-range EVs. In this wiki, it is cross-referenced with [[cathode-tier-comparison|High-Nickel vs Mid-Nickel vs LFP]], [[lfp-battery|LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) Battery]], and [[lmr-battery|Lithium Manganese Rich (LMR) Battery]].

## What To Remember
- More nickel increases cathode capacity but can lower thermal and structural stability if coating, doping, and electrolyte design are weak.
- LG Energy Solution sources discuss high-nickel NCMA and high-nickel cylindrical products as premium-performance options.
- The key tradeoff is energy density versus cost, safety margin, and process control.
