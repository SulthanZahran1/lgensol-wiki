# Anodeless Battery

## Definition
An anodeless battery starts without a host anode layer; lithium plates onto the anode current collector during charging.

## Why It Matters
- It can raise energy density but requires extremely stable plating, stripping, pressure, and electrolyte interfaces.
- In practical battery work, the term is useful because it connects a measurable behavior to design decisions in materials, manufacturing, BMS, or pack operation.
- Use it as a compact handle, then follow the related concept pages for the deeper electrochemistry or system design.
