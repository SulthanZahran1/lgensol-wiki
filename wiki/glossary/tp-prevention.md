# TP Prevention Technology (Thermal Propagation Prevention)

## Definition
TP prevention means thermal propagation prevention: design measures that stop one failing cell from spreading heat to neighbors.

## Why It Matters
- It is a pack-level safety topic involving barriers, vents, cooling, spacing, and detection.
- In practical battery work, the term is useful because it connects a measurable behavior to design decisions in materials, manufacturing, BMS, or pack operation.
- Use it as a compact handle, then follow the related concept pages for the deeper electrochemistry or system design.
