# CEI (Cathode Electrolyte Interphase)

## Definition
CEI means Cathode Electrolyte Interphase, the interphase that forms on cathode surfaces.

## Why It Matters
- A stable CEI reduces electrolyte oxidation, gas generation, and transition-metal dissolution.
- In practical battery work, the term is useful because it connects a measurable behavior to design decisions in materials, manufacturing, BMS, or pack operation.
