# Quaternary Battery

## Definition
A quaternary battery commonly refers to a four-element cathode family such as NCMA.

## Why It Matters
- Adding a fourth element can tune stability, cost, and energy density.
- In practical battery work, the term is useful because it connects a measurable behavior to design decisions in materials, manufacturing, BMS, or pack operation.
