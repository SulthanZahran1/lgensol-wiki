# CC/CV Charging (Constant Current/Constant Voltage Charging)

## Definition
CC/CV charging uses a constant-current phase followed by a constant-voltage phase.

## Why It Matters
- It is common for lithium-ion cells because it charges quickly early while limiting overvoltage near full charge.
- In practical battery work, the term is useful because it connects a measurable behavior to design decisions in materials, manufacturing, BMS, or pack operation.
- Use it as a compact handle, then follow the related concept pages for the deeper electrochemistry or system design.
