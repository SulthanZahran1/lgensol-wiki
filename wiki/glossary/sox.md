# SoX (State of X)

## Definition
SoX is a family term for state estimators such as SoC, SoH, state of power, and state of safety.

## Why It Matters
- It reflects the move from simple voltage monitoring to model-based battery diagnosis.
- In practical battery work, the term is useful because it connects a measurable behavior to design decisions in materials, manufacturing, BMS, or pack operation.
- Use it as a compact handle, then follow the related concept pages for the deeper electrochemistry or system design.

## In The LG Energy Solution Corpus
The term is used as a precise retrieval handle for posts about [[battery-management-system|BMS (Battery Management System)]], [[bmts|BMTS (B.around Total Solution)]], and [[cell-balancing|Cell Balancing]].
