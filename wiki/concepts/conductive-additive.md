# Conductive Additive (CNT)

## Core Idea
Conductive additive creates electron pathways through an electrode coating whose active material may be poorly conductive. It connects directly to [[pre-dispersion|Pre-Dispersion Process]], [[active-material|Active Material]], and [[battery-manufacturing|Battery Manufacturing Process Overview]].

## Technical Notes
- Carbon black, CNTs, and related carbon networks reduce resistance and improve power performance.
- Too little additive limits rate capability; too much displaces active material and lowers energy density.
- Conductive additive is co-designed with binder, active material, slurry mixing, coating, and calendering.
