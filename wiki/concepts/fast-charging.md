# Fast Charging

## Core Idea
Fast charging raises charging power while trying to avoid lithium plating, overheating, excessive SEI growth, and accelerated aging. It connects directly to [[46-series-battery|46-Series Cylindrical Battery (4680/4695)]], [[anode-materials|Anode Material]], and [[c-rate|C-rate (Current Rate / Charge-Discharge Rate)]].

## Technical Notes
- The limiting factors include anode diffusion, electrolyte transport, internal resistance, heat removal, BMS estimation, and cell balancing.
- Silicon anodes, electrode porosity, electrolyte additives, and thermal management can improve fast-charge capability.
- Fast charging is a system-level result, not a single material property.
