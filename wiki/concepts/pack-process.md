# Pack Process

## Core Idea
The pack process integrates cells into modules or packs with structure, sensing, thermal control, high-voltage paths, and protection. It connects directly to [[welding-process|Welding Process]], [[cell-to-pack|Cell-To-Pack (CTP)]], and [[46-series-battery|46-Series Cylindrical Battery (4680/4695)]].

## Technical Notes
- Pack design converts cell performance into usable EV or ESS performance.
- Key issues include crash safety, thermal propagation prevention, serviceability, current distribution, cooling, and BMS integration.
- CTP reduces module overhead but raises the importance of pack-level safety and diagnostics.
