# Welding Process

## Core Idea
Welding connects electrode tabs, current collectors, cans, modules, and busbars into low-resistance electrical paths. It connects directly to [[pack-process|Pack Process]], [[cell-to-pack|Cell-To-Pack (CTP)]], and [[tp-prevention|TP Prevention Technology (Thermal Propagation Prevention)]].

## Technical Notes
- Poor welds create heat, resistance variation, mechanical weakness, and safety risk.
- Battery welding must control material combinations such as aluminum and copper, thin foils, tabs, and busbars.
- It sits between assembly, pack process, current collectors, and end-of-line inspection.
