# LFP vs NCM Comparison

## Core Idea
A direct comparison of LFP and NCM battery chemistry for cost, safety, energy density, cycle life, and application fit. The useful way to read this comparison is through [[lfp-battery|LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) Battery]], [[ncm-battery|NCM (Ternary) Battery]], and [[cathode-materials|Cathode Material]].

## Decision Frame
- LFP generally wins on thermal stability, cost, and life; NCM generally wins on cell-level energy density.
- Pack architecture and vehicle segment can move the decision more than cell chemistry alone.
- ESS and affordable EVs often favor LFP, while long-range or performance EVs often favor NCM-family cathodes.
