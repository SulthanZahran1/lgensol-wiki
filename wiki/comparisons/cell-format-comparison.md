# Cell Form Factor Comparison (Cylindrical vs Pouch vs Prismatic)

## Core Idea
A comparison of cylindrical, pouch, and prismatic cell formats and the manufacturing/pack tradeoffs behind them. The useful way to read this comparison is through [[46-series-battery|46-Series Cylindrical Battery (4680/4695)]], [[azs-stacking|AZS (Advanced Z-Stacking)]], and [[assembly-process|Assembly Process]].

## Decision Frame
- Cylindrical cells benefit from mature high-speed winding and rigid cans.
- Pouch cells offer high packaging efficiency and flexible shapes but need external mechanical support.
- Prismatic cells integrate well into modules or packs but require precise case and swelling management.
