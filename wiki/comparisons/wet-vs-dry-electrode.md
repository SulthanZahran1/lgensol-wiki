# Wet vs Dry Electrode Process

## Core Idea
A comparison of conventional wet electrode manufacturing and emerging dry electrode processing. The useful way to read this comparison is through [[electrode-process|Electrode Process]], [[dry-electrode-process|Dry Electrode Process]], and [[battery-manufacturing|Battery Manufacturing Process Overview]].

## Decision Frame
- Wet processing is mature and uniform but solvent drying and recovery add cost, energy use, and floor space.
- Dry processing can lower cost and emissions, but uniform mixing, adhesion, porosity, and line speed are difficult.
- The comparison is central to manufacturing cost and next-generation process strategy.
